# Privacy Policy for Tiny Wins

_Last updated: 2026-06-11_

Tiny Wins is designed to be private by default.

## Data Collection

Tiny Wins does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is
no account system, no backend server, and no third-party analytics or
advertising SDKs.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only, using
Apple's SwiftData and UserDefaults frameworks:

- Your daily tiny win title and category
- The date and completion status of each daily win
- Your streak history (derived from the above)
- Your reminder time preference
- Whether you have completed onboarding

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any server we
operate, and we have no access to it.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, Tiny Wins schedules a single local notification on
your device using Apple's UserNotifications framework. No notification data
is sent to any server.

## Your Controls

From Settings, you can:

- Change or disable your daily reminder
- View this privacy explanation in-app
- Delete all local data permanently, at any time

## Children's Privacy

Tiny Wins does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children,
because it does not collect data at all.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included in the app's
documentation and an updated "Last updated" date will be shown above.
